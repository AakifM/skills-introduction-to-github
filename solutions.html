How Can We Stop Water Pollution?

  • Reduce plastic use: Use reusable bags and bottles.
    Whakamahia anō ngā mea: Pēnei i ngā pēke me ngā ipu inu.
  • Clean up waterways: Join community river cleanups.
    Whakapai i ngā awa: Hono ki ngā kaupapa whakapai hapori.
  • Educate others: Teach people why clean water is important.
    Whakaako i ētahi atu: Kia mōhio te katoa ki te hiranga o te wai mā.
